FAQ

Can customers upload their own images?

Yes. The custom designer can upload a main artwork image and multiple accent images, then submit the preview and original files for ForgeKeys AU to review.

Are you selling your own keyboards now?

Not yet. The site is structured for future own-brand drops, but the first commercial focus is custom keycaps and customisation services.

How does payment work?

Custom work is quote-based. Send your design first, approve the proof and price, then pay the deposit by PayID, bank transfer, or a secure card invoice link if requested.

What happens after I submit artwork?

ForgeKeys AU reviews the preview, original files, layout, print method, and factory requirements before replying with a quote or recommended changes.

Can photos and videos be used on the site?

Yes. Photos are best used as hero, case-study, and service proof. Videos can become short process clips, sound tests, or product loops.
