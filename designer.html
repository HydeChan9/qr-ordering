How to create a gallery-style keycap set
  1. Choose a layout such as 75%, 80% / TKL, or Alice.
  2. Upload one photo for the main mood, or several transparent PNG assets for details.
  3. Choose a Photo mode and press "Apply Photo Mode".
  4. For butterfly, logo, or novelty keys, click a single key, choose an Artwork placement, then press "Use Selected Image on This Key".
  5. Only open Fine tune if the image needs a small manual adjustment.

Factory artwork requirements
  • Preview uploads: JPG, PNG, or WebP, under 3 MB each.
  • Production source: transparent PNG, SVG, PDF, AI, or EPS where available.
  • Artwork target: 300 DPI at final print size, not enlarged from screenshots.
  • Keep important details inside the safe print area and allow bleed for edge-to-edge artwork.
  • Add Pantone or colour references when exact colour matching matters.
