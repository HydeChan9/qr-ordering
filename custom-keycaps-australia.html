Custom keycap FAQ

Can I upload my own photo?

Yes. You can upload artwork or photos for preview, then ForgeKeys AU reviews the source file quality before quoting production.

Can you make one single keycap?

Yes. Single keycaps, novelty keys, logo keys, spacebars, accent packs, and full layouts can all be quoted.

Can the preview go straight to a factory?

No. The preview is for quoting and visual direction. Final production files need template, safe area, material, profile, and print method checks.
