Custom keycaps and styled keyboards are quoted manually. If you do not have a quote or submission ID yet, send your artwork first. Once the proof and price are approved, use your ForgeKeys quote ID as the payment reference.

Before you pay

Payment details are provided only after your quote, proof, parts availability, and production scope are confirmed. Please do not send payment for an unapproved design or build reference.

  1. Submit your artwork, build reference, or custom brief.
  2. Wait for ForgeKeys AU to confirm the quote, timeline, and payment stage by email or message.
  3. Use the exact quote ID and amount shown on the approved quote.
  4. Production starts after payment clears and the final visual proof is approved.
